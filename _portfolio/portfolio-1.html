Non-collisional current sheets that form during magnetic reconnection can become unstable to the formation of plasmoids, enabling high reconnection rates.

In Granier et al. 2022, we investigate the instability conditions for plasmoid development in purely collisionless current sheets with a strong guide field and low plasma beta. The study was conducted using 2D simulations performed with the fluid solver SCOP3D, with a resolution of up to 2400x2400 points.
