   In 2017, I completed a Bachelor’s degree in mathematics at the University of Montpellier. I then completed a Master’s degree in fundamental physics in 2019 at the University of Bordeaux. In 2022, I received my PhD in plasma physics from the Côte d'Azur University and Politecnico di Torino for the thesis titled "New developments in the theory of current sheet instabilities in collisionless plasmas", advised by Dr. Emanuele Tassi and co-advised by Dr. Daniela Grasso.

 

    I am a postdoctoral research assistant under the joint supervision of Thierry Passot and Emanuele Tassi in the Fluid and Plasma Research Group.
