--- title: "Hybrid-Vlasov 3D3V Turbulence Simulation of Solar and Magnetospheric Plasmas" excerpt: "
" collection: portfolio --- In this work, we performed 3D3V hybrid-Vlasov (fully kinetic ions and fluid electrons) simulations of sub-ion-scale plasma turbulence with quasi-isotropic, compressible injection close to the ion scales. The goal of this study was to reproduce the typical conditions past the Earth’s bow shock, in the magnetosheath, to investigate electron-only reconnection and ion heating.
